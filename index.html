My Top Five Personal Values

Accountability

Why it's important: Accountability helps me stay responsible for my actions and builds trust with others.

How I developed it: I learned accountability through family expectations and school responsibilities.

What happens without it: A lack of accountability can damage relationships and credibility.

Communication

Why it's important: Communication allows me to share ideas clearly and work effectively with others.

How I developed it: Through teamwork in school, sports, and part-time jobs.

What happens without it: Poor communication can lead to misunderstandings and conflict.

Integrity

Why it's important: Integrity ensures that I act honestly and ethically in all situations.

How I developed it: My family and mentors taught me to always value honesty and fairness.

What happens without it: Without integrity, I risk losing my self-respect and othersâ€™ trust.

Perseverance

Why it's important: Perseverance motivates me to overcome challenges and achieve my goals.

How I developed it: Through sports, academics, and personal challenges that required resilience.

What happens without it: Giving up too soon would cause me to miss opportunities for growth.

Respect

Why it's important: Respect helps me build strong, positive relationships with others.

How I developed it: Learned from family values and cultural teachings emphasizing kindness.

What happens without it: Without respect, relationships can weaken and conflicts can arise.
